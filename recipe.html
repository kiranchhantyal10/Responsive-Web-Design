Quick & Delicious Noodle Recipe

Craving a quick and delicious meal? These homemade stir-fried noodles are packed with fresh vegetables, savory sauces, and irresistible flavor. Ready in just 15 minutes, this easy recipe is perfect for busy weeknights, lunch, or whenever you need a satisfying dish that's better than takeout. Enjoy a tasty bowl of noodles made right in your own kitchen. 🍜✨

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles (egg noodles, ramen noodles, or instant noodles)
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon chili sauce (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 spring onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the garlic and onion, then stir-fry for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the carrot and cabbage. Cook for 2–3 minutes, stirring frequently, until slightly tender.
  5. Stir in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and chili sauce. Mix well to coat the vegetables.
  6. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss everything together until evenly combined.
  7. Cook for another 2–3 minutes, allowing the noodles to absorb the flavors.
  8. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
