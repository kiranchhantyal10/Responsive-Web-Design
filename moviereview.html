3 Idiots

3 Idiots is a masterfully crafted coming-of-age comedy-drama that brilliantly satirizes the intense social pressures within the Indian education system. Directed by Rajkumar Hirani, the film follows two friends, Farhan and Raju, on a heartwarming quest to find their long-lost college roommate, the unconventional and brilliant Rancho. Through hilarious flashbacks to their demanding days at a prestigious engineering institute, the movie highlights Rancho's refreshing philosophy that true success stems from a passion for learning rather than rote memorization and societal compliance. Seamlessly blending humor, emotional depth, and catchy music, it delivers a powerful critique of mental health and rigid parental expectations while celebrating the enduring strength of true friendship.

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (9.2/10)

Cast Members

  • Aamir Khan as Ranchoddas "Rancho" Shamaldas Chanchad / Phunsukh Wangdu, the free-thinking, brilliant protagonist.
  • R. Madhavan as Farhan Qureshi, the narrator who secretly loves wildlife photography but studies engineering under parental pressure.
  • Sharman Joshi as Raju Rastogi, a student burdened by extreme poverty and family fears.
